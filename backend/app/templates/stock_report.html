Indicator Glossary

Indicator Term Definition & Interpretation
RSI (14) Relative Strength Index. A momentum oscillator measuring the speed and change of price movements. Readings above 70 suggest overbought levels, while below 30 suggest oversold levels.
MACD Moving Average Convergence Divergence. A trend-following momentum indicator displaying the relationship between two moving averages of price. Bullish crossovers suggest buy conditions.
ADX (14) Average Directional Index. Quantifies trend strength. Readings above 25 indicate a strong trending regime, while below 20 indicate rangebound or sideways behavior.
ATR (14) Average True Range. A volatility indicator measuring price dispersion. High ATR ratio indicates wide daily range shifts.
Value at Risk (VaR) Measures the maximum expected loss over a specific horizon at a given confidence level under typical market conditions.
